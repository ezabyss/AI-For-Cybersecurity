# 🔁 Agentic AI Loop: How Autonomous AI Thinks

---

# 📌 1. Big Picture

👉 Agentic AI is not just automation  

👉 It is:

**A thinking system**

---

## ⚠️ Key Idea

👉 Agentic AI follows a continuous loop:

**Plan → Act → Evaluate → Adjust**

---

# 🧠 2. Core Idea 

👉 **Agentic AI uses a self-directed loop to break goals into actions, learn from results, and continuously improve**

---

# 🎯 3. Step 1: Goal

👉 Not a simple task  

👉 A high-level objective  

---

## 🧠 Example

- "Find vulnerabilities in system"  
- "Map company infrastructure"  

---

👉 AI decides:
**What needs to be done**

---

# 📋 4. Step 2: Planning

👉 AI breaks goal into:

- Subtasks  
- Steps  
- Strategy  

---

## 🧠 Example Plan

- Gather data  
- Scan network  
- Check open ports  
- Analyze services  

---

👉 Important:

**Steps are NOT pre-programmed**

---

# ⚡ 5. Step 3: Execution

👉 AI performs actions:

- Calls APIs  
- Scans systems  
- Reads data  
- Writes scripts  

---

👉 Works like:

**Real human operator**

---

# 🔍 6. Step 4: Evaluation

👉 AI checks:

- Did it work?  
- Was result useful?  
- Any error?  

---

👉 If failure:

- Identify reason  
- Adjust approach  

---

# 🔁 7. Step 5: Loop (MOST IMPORTANT)

👉 AI repeats:

**Plan → Act → Evaluate → Adjust**

---

👉 Continuously improves  

👉 Never stops
