# 🏢 AI Vendor Risk & Due Diligence

---

# 📌 1. Big Picture

👉 Most AI is NOT built in-house  

👉 Organizations use:

- Third-party AI tools  
- Vendors for automation, analytics, hiring, support  

---

👉 Problem:

**You are trusting external AI with your business**

---

## ⚠️ Key Idea

👉 AI vendor ≠ just software  

👉 It is:

**A decision-making system with access to your data**

---

# 🧠 2. Core Concept

👉 AI vendor risk =

**Risk from external AI systems handling your data and decisions**

---

# ⚠️ 3. Why This is Dangerous

👉 AI vendors may:

- Access sensitive data  
- Make automated decisions  
- Influence business outcomes  

---

👉 And often:

**You don’t fully know how they work**

---

# 🚨 4. The Transparency Problem

👉 Many vendors don’t clearly disclose:

- Training data sources  
- Data usage practices  
- Model decision logic  
- Audit capabilities  

---

👉 Result:

**Black box AI**

---

# 🔴 5. Major Red Flags
👉 Watch out if vendor:

- ❌ Can’t explain training data  
- ❌ Says “we use public data” (vague)  
- ❌ Has unclear privacy policy  
- ❌ Can’t isolate your data  
- ❌ Lacks audit logs  
- ❌ Avoids compliance questions  

---

👉 Rule:

**No transparency = No trust**

---

# 🔍 6. Key Questions to Ask Vendors

---

## 📊 Data Handling:

- What data do you collect?  
- Where is it stored?  
- Is it used for training?  
- How is it deleted?  

---

## 🧠 Model Behavior:

- How are decisions made?  
- Is bias tested?  
- Is model explainable?  
- How often is it updated?  

---

## ⚖️ Compliance:

- GDPR support?  
- HIPAA support?  
- Audit logs available?  
- Can data be traced per client?  

---

## 🚨 Incident Handling:

- What happens if AI fails?  
- Who is responsible?  
- What is response time?  

---

# ⚖️ 7. Legal Reality

👉 If AI causes damage:

**Your organization is still responsible**

---

👉 Vendor ≠ liability transfer  

---

# 🧑‍💼 8. Role of Leadership

👉 AI procurement needs:

- Executive oversight  
- Security involvement  
- Legal & compliance review  

---

👉 Treat vendor like:

**Hiring a decision-maker**

---

# 🧩 9. Governance in Procurement

---

## ✅ Must include:

- Risk assessment  
- Vendor evaluation  
- Data protection checks  
- Compliance verification  
- Ongoing monitoring  

---

# 📜 10. Contracts & Agreements

👉 Should clearly define:

- Data ownership  
- Data usage  
- Model behavior  
- Audit rights  
- Incident response  

---

👉 Important:

**No hidden clauses**

---

# 🔄 11. Subprocessors Risk

👉 Vendor may use:

- Third-party services  

---

👉 Must ensure:

- Transparency  
- Control  
- Consent  

---

# 💀 12. Risks of Poor Vendor Selection

👉 Leads to:

- Data leaks  
- Compliance violations  
- Biased decisions  
- Reputational damage  

---

# 🧠 13. Memory Trick

👉 AI Vendor Risk =

**Data + Decisions + Trust**

---

👉 Golden rule:

**If you don’t understand it → don’t trust it**

---

# 🎯 ONE-LINE SUMMARY

👉 **AI vendor risk arises when third-party AI systems handle sensitive data and decisions without transparency, requiring strict due diligence and governance**
