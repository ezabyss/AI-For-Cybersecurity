👉 Cyber attacks are no longer about hacking systems  

👉 They are about:
**Hacking human decisions**

---

## ⚠️ Key Idea

👉 **Attackers don’t break into devices — they break into your thinking**

# 🧠 2. Core Idea (One Line)

👉 **AI uses your behavior and psychology to manipulate your decisions**

- Who you are  
- How you think  
- How you react

- Social media posts 📱  
- Online activity 🌐  
- Work platforms 💼  
- Messages & interactions 💬

## 🧠 What AI Learns

- Your personality  
- Your communication style  
- Your habits  
- Your emotional triggers
