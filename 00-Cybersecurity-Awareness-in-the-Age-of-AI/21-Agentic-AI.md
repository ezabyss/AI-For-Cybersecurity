# 🤖 Agentic AI: Autonomous Intelligence in Cybersecurity

---

# 📌 1. Big Picture

👉 Old AI:

- Waits for instructions  
- Completes one task  
- Stops  

---

👉 New AI:

**Acts on its own**

---

# ⚠️ Key Shift

👉 From:
"Do this task"

👉 To:
**"What should I do next?"**

---

# 🧠 2. Core Idea (One Line)

👉 **Agentic AI = AI that can plan, decide, and act independently to achieve goals**

---

# ⚙️ 3. What Makes Agentic AI Different

👉 Agentic AI can:

- Set goals 🎯  
- Break tasks into steps  
- Execute actions  
- Adapt based on results  

---

👉 In simple terms:

**Think → Plan → Act → Learn**

---

# 🔁 4. Agent Workflow (VERY IMPORTANT)

1. Goal given  
2. Task breakdown  
3. Execute steps  
4. Evaluate results  
5. Adjust strategy  
6. Repeat  

---

👉 Loop continues until goal is achieved
