# 🧠 2. Core Idea (One Line)

# 📱 3. Smishing (SMS Phishing)

## 📌 What It Is

👉 Phishing through text messages  

---

## 🔥 Modern Smishing (AI-Powered)

- Short  
- Relevant  
- Personalized  

---

## 📩 Example

> "Hi Abhishek, your package is delayed. Track here: [link]"

---

## 🔍 Why It Works

- Uses your name  
- Feels urgent  
- Looks normal  
- Easy to click  

---

## ⚠️ Key Insight

👉 Phones = personal → higher trust  

---

# 🤖 4. AI Chatbot Attacks (VERY IMPORTANT)

## 📌 What Happens After You Click

👉 You are redirected to:
- Fake support chat  
- AI chatbot  

---

## 🧠 What Chatbot Does

- Asks for:
  - Account ID  
  - Password  
  - Verification details  

---

## 🔁 AI Capabilities

- Responds in real-time  
- Adjusts tone  
- Answers questions  
- Mimics human behavior  

---

## ⚠️ Danger

👉 Feels like real support  
👉 But it’s fake  

---

# 🧍 5. Real-World Scenario

## Step-by-Step Attack

1. Receive SMS  
2. Click link  
3. Chatbot appears  
4. You share details  
5. Attack successful  

---

# 🖼️ 6. Image-Based Phishing

## 📌 What It Is

👉 Phishing using images instead of text

---

## 🔥 Examples

- Fake QR codes  
- Fake invoices  
- Fake login screens  
- Fake app interfaces  

---

## ⚠️ Why Dangerous

👉 Humans trust visuals more  

👉 AI makes:
- Perfect designs  
- Real-looking interfaces  

---

# 🧠 7. Visual Deception

👉 AI can generate:

- Fake screenshots  
- Fake login pages  
- Fake security alerts  

---

## 💡 Key Insight

👉 **You may not be able to visually detect fake vs real**

---

# 🛡️ 12. Defense Strategy

## 🔐 What You Must Do

- Do NOT click unknown links  
- Verify through official apps/websites  
- Avoid sharing data in chatbots  
- Be cautious of QR codes  
- Slow down before acting
