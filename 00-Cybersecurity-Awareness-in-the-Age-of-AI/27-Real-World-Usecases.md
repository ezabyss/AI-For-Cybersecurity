# 💀 7. Malicious Use (VERY IMPORTANT)

👉 Attackers are using agentic AI  

---

## 🚨 Capabilities:

- Crawl for leaked credentials  
- Test login systems  
- Rotate IPs  
- Exfiltrate data  
- Delete logs  

---

👉 Fully automated attack  

---

# 🚨 8. Warning Signs

👉 Signs of unsafe AI behavior:

---

## 🔍 Look for:

- Unusual automation patterns  
- Actions no one scheduled  
- AI making overconfident decisions  
- Human-like but “too perfect” activity  
- Unexpected system-to-system traffic  
- Tasks chaining unexpectedly  
- AI not stopping when it should  

---

👉 Key idea:

**Autonomy without control**

---

# ⚠️ 9. Why This Happens

👉 AI follows instructions blindly  

👉 It:

- Lacks real understanding  
- Lacks judgment  
- Cannot know limits  

---

# 🧑‍💻 10. What Organizations Need

👉 To stay safe:

---

## ✅ Must have:

- Governance frameworks  
- Clear boundaries  
- Monitoring systems  
- Audit logs  
- Fail-safes / kill switch  
- Human oversight  

---
