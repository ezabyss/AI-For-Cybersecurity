# 🤖 AI-Powered Cyber Threats: The New Era of Phishing & Attacks

---

# 📌 1. Big Picture

👉 Cybersecurity has changed.

**Old attacks:**
- Easy to detect  
- Poor grammar  
- Fake-looking emails  

**New attacks (AI-powered):**
- Personal  
- Realistic  
- Highly convincing  

👉 Goal:
Understand how **AI is changing cyber threats**

---

# 🕰️ 2. Then vs Now (VERY IMPORTANT)

## 🔹 Old Phishing (Around 2010)

Example:
> "Your PayPal account is locked. Click here."

### 🔍 Signs:
- Bad grammar  
- Fake email address  
- Poor design  

👉 Easy to detect ❌

---

## 🔹 Modern AI Phishing (Today)

Example:
> "Hey, can you process this transfer before lunch? I'll explain later."

### 🔍 Signs:
- Uses your name/nickname  
- References real meeting  
- Sounds like your manager  

👉 Very hard to detect ⚠️

---

# ⚠️ 3. What Changed?

## 🧠 Before:
- Manual attacks  
- Slow  
- Generic  

## 🤖 Now:
- AI-generated  
- Fast  
- Personalized

# 🔥 4. Capabilities of AI in Cyber Attacks

## 🗣️ 2. Voice Cloning

## 💬 3. AI Chatbots
- Real-time conversations  
- Trick users interactively  

---

## 🎥 4. Deepfake Videos
- Fake but realistic videos  
- Hard to detect  

---

## 🦠 5. Advanced Malware
- Changes behavior  
- Evades detection

# 🧍 7. Real-World SOC Scenario

Employee receives:

> "Hey, can you urgently process payment? We discussed this yesterday."

---

- Uses real context  
- Sounds natural

## 🚨 Risk:
