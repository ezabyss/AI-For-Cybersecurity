# 🚨 Incident Response for Employees (First 5 Minutes Matter)

---

# 📌 1. Big Picture

👉 Suspicious activity can happen anytime  

- Phishing email  
- Strange login alert  
- Unexpected file/link  
- Accidental click  

---

👉 What you do NEXT matters most  

---

## ⚠️ Key Idea

👉 **First 5 minutes = Damage control**

---

# 🧠 2. Core Concept

👉 You don’t need to be an expert  

👉 You need to:

**Respond correctly and quickly**

---

# 🛑 3. Step 1: STOP Immediately

👉 If something feels off:

- Do NOT click  
- Do NOT open attachments  
- Do NOT enter credentials  
- Do NOT investigate yourself  

---

👉 Rule:

**Pause = Protection**

---

# 📢 4. Step 2: REPORT Immediately

👉 Use your organization’s method:

- Report phishing button  
- IT/security email  
- Slack channel / form  

---

👉 Important:

- Don’t wait  
- Don’t assume others will report  
- Don’t overthink  

---

👉 Key idea:

**Early reporting = Less damage**

---

# 😬 5. Step 3: Be Honest (CRITICAL)

👉 If you clicked or entered info:

**Say it immediately**

---

👉 Why?

- Faster containment  
- Less spread  
- Better recovery  

---

👉 Security team is there to:

**Help, not blame**

---

# 🔌 6. Step 4: Disconnect (If Needed)

👉 If you suspect malware:

- Disconnect internet (Wi-Fi/Ethernet)  

---

👉 But:

- ❌ Do NOT shut down system  
- ❌ Do NOT restart  

---

👉 Reason:

**Preserve evidence for investigation**

---

# 🧾 7. Step 5: Do NOT Delete Anything

👉 Avoid:

- Deleting emails  
- Clearing history  
- Removing files  

---

👉 Why?

**Logs = Evidence**

---

# 🔄 8. Step 6: Cooperate with IT/Security

👉 If asked:

- Respond quickly  
- Reset credentials  
- Provide access  

---

👉 This is:

**Containment, not punishment**

---

# ⚠️ 9. Biggest Mistake

👉 NOT the click  

👉 But:

- Delaying report  
- Hiding mistake  
- Trying to fix alone  

---

👉 Result:

**Small issue → Big breach**

---

# 🔁 10. Response Mindset

👉 Security is not just prevention  

👉 It is:

**Detection + Response**

---

👉 Goal:

**Turn mistakes into controlled events**

---

# 🧠 11. Memory Trick

👉 Response steps:

**Stop → Report → Be Honest → Disconnect → Preserve → Cooperate**

---

👉 Short version:

**Pause → Report → Protect**

---

# 🎯 ONE-LINE SUMMARY

👉 **Effective incident response requires employees to stop interaction, report immediately, and cooperate with security teams to contain threats quickly**
