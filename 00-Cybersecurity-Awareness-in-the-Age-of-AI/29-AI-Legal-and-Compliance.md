# 🌍 3. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) 
>(EU Data Protection Law)

👉 Protects:

**Personal data of EU citizens**

---

## 🔍 Applies when:

- Handling names  
- Emails  
- Customer IDs  
- Any identifiable data  

---

👉 Even if company is NOT in EU  

---

## ⚠️ Requirements:

- Legal basis (consent, contract, etc.)  
- Data protection  
- Limited use (purpose-specific)  

---

## ❗ Important Right:

👉 Users can:

- Ask for explanation  
- Challenge AI decisions  

---

👉 Example:

AI rejects loan → must explain why  

---

# 🏥 4. HIPAA (The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) 
> (Healthcare Data - USA)

👉 Protects:

**PHI (Protected Health Information)**

---

## 🧠 Includes:

- Patient records  
- Treatment details  
- Health history  
- Billing info  

---

## ⚠️ Key Rule:

👉 Most public AI tools are:

**NOT HIPAA compliant**

---

👉 Risk:

- Using ChatGPT for patient data = violation  

---

👉 Safe only if:

- Approved by compliance team  
- Proper agreements in place  

---
