# 🕵️ Shadow AI: The Hidden Risk in Modern Organizations

---

# 📌 1. Big Picture

👉 Organizations have:
- Approved tools ✅  
- Unapproved tools ❌  

👉 This gap is called:
**Shadow IT**

---

## 🔥 New Problem

👉 Now we have:
**Shadow AI**

---

# 🧠 2. Core Idea

👉 **Shadow AI = Using AI tools without approval, creating hidden security risks**

---

# ⚔️ 3. Shadow IT vs Shadow AI (VERY IMPORTANT)

| Concept | Shadow IT | Shadow AI |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Risk Type | Storage & access | Data + content + processing |
| Visibility | Sometimes detectable | Mostly invisible |
| Example | Dropbox, apps | ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot |

---

# 🤖 4. What is Shadow AI?

👉 Use of AI tools without:
- Approval  
- Security review  
- IT awareness  

---

## 📱 Examples

- ChatGPT  
- Google Gemini  
- Copilot  
- AI plugins/extensions  

---

# ⚠️ 5. Why People Use It

👉 Because it is:
- Fast ⚡  
- Easy  
- Helpful  
- Productive  

---

## 💡 Reality

👉 Employees are not careless  
👉 They are trying to **work better**

---

# 💀 6. The Hidden Risk (MOST IMPORTANT)

👉 Every prompt = data exposure  

---

## 🧠 Example

Employee pastes:
- Company report  
- Customer data  
- Internal project  

👉 Into AI tool  

---

## ⚠️ Risk

- Data stored externally  
- Data processed in cloud  
- Data possibly reused  

---

# 🔥 7. New Type of Risk

👉 Not just:
"Where data goes"

👉 But:
**What happens to data**

---

## 🧠 Questions to Ask

- Is data stored?  
- Is it used to train AI?  
- Is it shared with others?  
- Who has access?  

---

👉 Most users don’t know ❌  

---

# 🌐 8. Why Shadow AI is Hard to Detect

👉 It:

- Runs in cloud ☁️  
- Uses browser/apps 🌐  
- Leaves no clear logs  

---

👉 It is NOT:
- In firewall  
- In network monitoring  

---

👉 It lives in:
**Prompts, copy-paste, and queries**

---

# ⚠️ 9. Real-World Scenario

## Situation:

Employee uses ChatGPT to:

> "Rewrite this internal company report"

---

## 🔍 What Happens

- Sensitive data uploaded  
- Stored externally  
- Possibly used by AI system  

---

## 🚨 Result

👉 Data leakage without realizing  

---

# 📈 10. Why This is Dangerous

👉 It feels:
- Safe  
- Helpful  
- Normal  

---

👉 But it is:
**Invisible risk**

---

# ⚠️ 11. Key Shift in Cybersecurity

👉 Before:
"Control systems and access"

👉 Now:
**Control behavior and usage**

---

# 🛡️ 12. Defense Strategy

## 🔐 Awareness is #1 defense

---

## 🔑 What You Must Do

- Never paste sensitive data into AI tools  
- Use only approved tools  
- Understand privacy policies  
- Think before using AI  

---

# 💭 13. Memory Tricks

👉 Shadow AI = invisible risk  

👉 Prompt = data exposure  

---

👉 AI tools = helpful BUT risky  

---

👉 Biggest danger:
**Convenience**

---

# 🧠 14. Final Understanding

- Shadow AI is growing rapidly  
- Risk comes from user behavior  
- Small actions can cause big leaks  

---

# 🎯 ONE-LINE SUMMARY

👉 **Shadow AI creates hidden risks by exposing sensitive data through unapproved AI tools, making user awareness the strongest defense**
