- No manual work needed

- Continuous 🔁

# 🎯 4. Personalization (TARGETING)

## 📌 What It Means

👉 AI uses your:
- Social media  
- Company info  
- Online behavior  

---

## 🧠 What AI Learns

- Your role  
- Your writing style  
- Your team structure  

---

## 📩 Example

> "Hey Abhishek, can you review the security report we discussed yesterday?"

👉 Looks real  
👉 Feels normal  
👉 Built using your data

## ⚠️ Danger

- It feels familiar  
- It uses real context

# 🎭 5. Deception (MOST DANGEROUS)

## 📌 What It Means

👉 AI can fake:

- Voices 🗣️  
- Videos 🎥  
- Messages 💬  

---

## 🧍 Real Scenario

👉 You get a call:

> "Hey, this is your manager. Approve this payment urgently."

👉 Voice sounds real  
👉 Tone is correct  
👉 Urgency is high  

❗ But it's fake  

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

👉 AI is not just tricking your eyes  
👉 It is tricking your **trust**
