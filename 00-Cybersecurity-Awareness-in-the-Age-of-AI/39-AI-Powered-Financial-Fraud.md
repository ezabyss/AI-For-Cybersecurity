# 🛡️ 9. Defense Strategy (CRITICAL)

---

## 🔐 1. Dual Verification (MOST IMPORTANT)

👉 Always verify:

- Payment changes  
- New vendor requests  

---

👉 Use:

- Phone call  
- Different email  
- Internal confirmation  

---

👉 NEVER rely on same email thread  

---

## 🧠 2. Slow Thinking

👉 If message is:

- Urgent  
- Emotional  
- Time-pressured  

---

👉 PAUSE  

---

👉 AI often uses urgency to manipulate  

---

## 🔍 3. Behavior-Based Detection

👉 Ask:

- Was this expected?  
- Is timing unusual?  
- Does request match normal process?
